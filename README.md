# MyAspen

**MyAspen** is a command-line interface (CLI) tool designed to simplify balance management for individuals receiving weekly allowances, including those using Aspen cards. Originally built to help asylum seekers manage their Aspen card balances without the hassle of phone calls or ATM visits, MyAspen is now equipped with additional features that make it useful for anyone receiving regular payments, such as students, prepaid cardholders, or individuals with weekly stipends.

## Key Features

- **Manual Balance Tracking**: Easily update and keep track of your current balance and weekly allowance, ideal for managing prepaid cards or regular allowances.
- **Expense Management**: Log your spending to maintain an accurate overview of your available funds.
- **Automated Notifications**: Receive updates every Monday with your new balance. You can choose to be notified via Pushbullet or email.
- **Versatile Use**: Suitable for asylum seekers, students, prepaid cardholders, or anyone receiving regular payments.
- **Secure and Offline**: No sensitive information is shared online. Users manually input data, ensuring complete control and privacy.
- **Open Source**: Released under the GNU General Public License, encouraging community involvement and enhancements.
